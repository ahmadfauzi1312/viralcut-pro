Privacy Policy — ViralCut Pro

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

ViralCut Pro collects only the information necessary to provide our content automation service. This includes your social media account credentials (stored securely), content preferences, and scheduling settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to operate the ViralCut Pro service — finding viral content, editing clips, and scheduling uploads to your connected social media accounts. We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. TikTok Data Usage

When you connect your TikTok account, ViralCut Pro requests permission to upload videos on your behalf. We only access the minimum required scopes: user.info.basic and video.upload/video.publish. We do not store your TikTok credentials beyond what is needed for the upload functionality.

4. Data Storage

Your settings and preferences are stored securely on our servers. Social media access tokens are encrypted and stored only for the duration needed to perform scheduled uploads.

5. Data Deletion

You can disconnect any social media account at any time from the Accounts page. This will immediately revoke our access and delete stored tokens for that platform.

6. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us at: viralcutpro@gmail.com
